## Key Findings Summary

- **8.6%** of the 319,795 surveyed individuals reported being diagnosed with heart disease
- **Older age groups (60+)** show significantly higher prevalence rates
- **Smoking** and **diabetes** are the strongest correlated risk factors
- **Physical inactivity** is present in a disproportionate share of heart disease cases
- **Race disparities** are visible — certain groups show higher prevalence independent of lifestyle factors
